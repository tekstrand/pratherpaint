Ultimate Nano Protection

C2 Guard is a non-toxic product waterproofer designed for use on unsealed wood and concrete surfaces. C2 Guard contains a flexible polymer that transforms and stabilizes the cellular structure of the wood to prevent water intrusion and dry rot from within. This enhanced cellular structure resists water penetration, prevents cracking, splitting, or warping, while at the same time reducing the effects of mold and mildew.

C2 Guard for Wood

Seals your wooden surfaces for years using nano-technology. Absorbs so deep into the wood that it becomes part of the cellular structure, creating a flexible polymer barrier. Prevents cracking, warping, splitting, and reduces mold and mildew.

C2 Guard for Masonry

Seals masonry and brick surfaces for years using nano-technology. Penetrates deeper than any other product on the market to prevent corrosion, spalling, cracks, and reduces mold and mildew.
