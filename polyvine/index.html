Polyvine Tropical Scumble

The original clear water based transparent glaze for decorative effects. Unique in providing up to one hour working time. Simply mix with satin or flat latex paint, or color with colorisers to produce stunning decorative paint effects. Tropical Scumble glaze slows down the drying time of emulsion paint so you can use it to make all kinds of paint effects.

Ideal for
  • walls
  • doors
  • furniture
  • kitchen cabinets
  • most hard surfaces
Unique Qualities
  • easy to use
  • water wash up
  • water based
  • low odor
  • up to 1 hour working time

Polyvine Classic Color

This clear water based acrylic glaze when colored with emulsion paint produces semi translucent colored decorative paint effects. Because it uses your choice of latex paint as the colorant it eliminates the need to mix colors. Classic Color acrylic all purpose water based extender is unique in providing up to one hour working time. Plan the effects in two colors, the first is the base coat, often white, the second coat is the colored glaze. Classic Color has amazing adhesion capabilities and durability for long lasting applications.

Ideal for
  • walls including kitchens and bathrooms
  • doors
  • furniture
  • kitchen cabinets
  • built-ins
  • cupboard
Unique Qualities
  • one hour working time
  • semi-translucent
  • water based
  • paint effects

Polyvine Decorators Varnish

A clear acrylic coating formulated to protect a wide range of surfaces. Water resistant, stain resistant, heat resistant, and vapor permeable. Seals and protects wallpaper, latex paint, plaster, fabrics, wood, and decorative paint finishes.

Available in
  • Dead flat
  • Satin
  • Gloss

Polyvine Crackle Glaze

Designed to produce consistent cracking of emulsion paint. This acrylic medium creates dramatic two colour aged effects.

The crackle glaze is applied over an emulsion base coat which will be the colour of the cracks. An emulsion top coat in the chosen colour is then applied over the glaze. The top coat cracks to reveal the base coat.

Ideal for
  • furniture
  • skirting/architrave
  • pottery
  • picture frames
  • decorative aged effects
Unique Qualities
  • easy to apply
  • water wash up
  • water based
  • low odor
  • quick drying
