ML Campbell UltraStar

M.L. Campbell UltraStar Water Borne Clear Finishes represents the best of the dawning age of aqueous coatings. UltraStar Water Borne coating systems offer a Water Borne clear finishing system that has the performance, application, durability and quality normally found only in the best solvent-based precatalyzed lacquers. Plus, UltraStar offers the added benefit of meeting the most stringent environmental concerns.

Unique Advantages

  • Acrylic/Polyurethane copolymer formulation.
  • Exceeds conventional Nitrocellulose clear lacquers in durability-hardness/abrasion resistant-flexibility-chemical resistance.
  • VOC Compliant, meets or exceeds all current U.S. regulations, in addition, formulated to contain no substances on U.S. regulated Toxic Pollutant List.
  • Available in Gloss, Satin, Dull and a Sanding Sealer.
  • Non-flammable, non-toxic, helps you meet insurance, fire and safety requirements without sacrificing product performance.
  • Ease of application - sprays like a conventional Nitrocellulose lacquer. Has high solids (27%) and fast lacquer dry.
