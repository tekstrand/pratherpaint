ML Campbell Polystar

This waterborne topcoat is a unique blend of acrylic and polyurethane copolymers. For exceptional moisture resistance and durable film properties on a wide range of interior surfaces. Over 800 colors in gloss or satin luster!

Unique Advantages

  • Easy to apply - sprays like conventional coatings.
  • Fast dry - dries like conventional coatings.
  • Nonflammable - nontoxic.
  • Eliminates disposal problems.
  • Low odor - provides an improved work environment.
  • Offers the performance of pre-catalyzed systems.
  • One component - no mixing required.
  • Low VOC - only 260 grams per liter.
  • High solids - 40%.
