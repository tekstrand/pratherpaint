ML Campbell Magnamax

M.L. Campbell MagnaMax™ is a technological breakthrough for pre-catalyzed lacquer clear finishing systems. This hi-solids water white formula was designed and developed to maximize the durability and overall performance available from pre-catalyzed technology.

  • Ultra low formaldehyde-meets European E1 Standard
  • Low in VOC-only 625 grams per liter
  • 33% solids-only 2 coats required
  • Water white formula
  • Self-sealing formula with outstanding sanding properties
  • Excellent chemical resistance-See Below
  • Exceptional film build
  • Excellent adhesion on both closed and open grain substrate
