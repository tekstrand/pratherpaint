ML Campbell Resistant

Catalyzed Pigmented Varnish. M.L. Campbell Resistant is a high-performance coating system that truly adds value to your furniture and cabinets by producing a finish that combines outstanding looks with exceptional durability. Resistant is a world class, two component, amino-alkyd finishing system specially designed for use on furniture, kitchen and bathroom cabinets, and display fixtures.

Unique Advantages

  • The best of North American and European formulating technology.
  • Formulated with No HAPs solvents to help you comply with environmental regulations.
  • Ultra low formaldehyde formulation which meets world E-1 standards for a better work environment.
  • No recoat windows - quick cure cycle.
  • Fast dry - dries much faster than most conventional catalyzed varnish products.
  • Exceptional durability and resistance to water,household chemicals and wear by usage.
  • Available in three lusters - gloss, semi-gloss and satin in a white, opaque or clear base.
  • Available in over 800 popular colors.
  • Packaged pre-reduced - just add catalyst.
  • Excellent scratch and mar resistance.
