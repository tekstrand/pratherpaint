Behlen Brothers Jet Spray

Jet Spray™ Toners are formulated to blend spot repairs to the surrounding surface. These toners are produced from pigments dissolved into nitrocellulose lacquer. They are essential for repairs on pieces that were toned with a pigmented toner by the manufacturer. They are very resistant to color fade from UV exposure. They may also be used as the toning agent when finishing small projects.
