Oz® Cream Polish

Oz® Cream Polish boasts a concentrated formula that cleans, polishes and protects. Just apply, wipe, and buff up to a beautiful sheen. Oz® Cream Polish is excellent for all types of smooth surfaces: wood, marble, tile, leather, and painted or chrome metal. Oz® Cream Polish is non-flammable, non-abrasive, greaseless, quick drying, and contains no silicones. Oz® is now also available in a spill proof, environmentally safe pump bottle. When all of the great features of Oz® Polish are combined with a refillable pump bottle, one truly has the "ALL-IN-ONE" furniture cream polish.
