About Prather Paint

  1. Home
  2. About Prather Paint

A big thank you from the entire Ekstrand Family for your patronage over the past 42 years. As of June 21st, 2016 Prather Paint has officially closed it's doors. We recommend US Paint Supply for your paint supply needs.

One of the last truly independent paint stores, Prather Paint provides quality products with knowledgeable and personalized service. Our customers come not only from the Chicago suburbs & North Shore but from a five-state area because Prather's inventory includes paints and wood finishes that can't be found anywhere else. Whether you are a home owner, a decorator, or a contractor, Prather will be able to provide you with specialized service and help you find the pigment of your imagination!
