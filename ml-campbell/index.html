Clawlock

Clawlock Primer/Undercoater is a high performance, high solids (55%), 2 component postcatalyzed (amino-alkyd) primer/undercoater for furniture, store fixtures and cabinets. Clawlock provides an excellent base coat for the start of a high performance pigmented finishing system. Based on European technology, it is formulated to meet world Care E-1 performance standards. Its' high solids, fast drying characteristics lend itself to good production properties and excellent sanding characteristics. Especially developed for filling the open pores on MDF Board, it is also suitable for a high performance/undercoater on all types of woods.

Unique Advantages

  • Ultra high solids (55%) for fast filling of MDF Board and open pores on wood.
  • High performance cross-link primer, that provides an excellent base for almost all finish coats.
  • Formulated with no aromatic or ketone solvents (HAPS) provides a better work environment and meets or exceeds current environmental regulations.

ML Campbell Duravar

Post-Catalyzed Lacquer. M.L. Campbell Duravar Plus is an ultra-high performance coating system designed to be used on kitchen and bath cabinets and institutional, household, office and laboratory furniture. The unique two component amino-alkyd, self-sealing formula adds value to your furniture and cabinets by producing a finish with outstanding depth, clarity and aesthetics, while providing superior durability and resistance to chemicals and moisture.

Unique Advantages

  • The best of European and North American formulation technology to help you comply with environmental regulations.
  • Formulated with No HAPs solvents.
  • Ultra low formaldehyde formulation which meets world E-1 standards for a better work environment.
  • High solids, 42% - just 2 coats will build an excellent finish.
  • Self-seal, 1 - product finishing system, no sealer necessary.
  • Sands better than most sealers.
  • No recoat window - quick cure cycle.
  • Fast dry - dries much faster than most conventional catalyzed varnish products.

ML Campbell Woodsong

Woodsong Stain
Fast drying spray and wipe wood stains that can be recoated with lacquers or other industrial wood finishes. This fast dry formula (30-60 minutes) applies by spraying and wiping the stain directly on the wood to be finished.

Woodsong Microton Toners
Woodsong Microton Toners were developed to act as the first step in staining light colored woods (oak, pine, birch etc.) to a darker color such as mahogany. When used as a first stain coat, this spray-only toner uniforms the tone of the wood's color, making an even base for the application of Woodsong Microton Toner.

Woodsong Traditional Glaze
WoodSong Traditional Glaze was developed to add depth to 3-dimensional surfaces, imitate aging, and create other decorative patterns on interior wood surfaces. This product exhibits excellent workability, as well as fast and easy recoating. Over 300 custom colors of WoodSong Traditional Glaze are available.
